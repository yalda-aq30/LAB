Safety Guidelines

Safety is our top priority. All team members must follow the established protocols to ensure a secure working environment. This includes wearing appropriate protective gear, maintaining clear walkways in the warehouse, and immediately reporting any faulty equipment. Our collective commitment to these safety standards protects not only ourselves but also our partners and the community we serve.
