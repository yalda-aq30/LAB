Company Policies

Our company is committed to maintaining the highest standards of security to protect our international partners, our local delivery systems, and the privacy of the customers we serve. All employees, including permanent staff and seasonal hires, are required to use unique, individual credentials for all API-integrated systems and must never share passwords or leave active sessions unattended. Because we share a warehouse space with global partners, it is mandatory that our designated storage area remains organized and that all high-value parcels are moved to the secure zone immediately upon arrival. Any digital or physical security breach, including the loss of a handheld device or a package, must be reported to the office manager within one hour to ensure we meet our contractual notification obligations.
